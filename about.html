Hi! I'm Diana Cao.


Born and raised in Houston, Texas, I am currently a student in the University Of Houston's Graphic Design program pursing a Bachelors of Fine Arts. I am a graphic designer who loves to design with a sense of daintiness and elegance; in particular, I am interested in experimental design and print production. When I am not designing, I love to read Webtoons, take walks, and spend time with my loves ones!
