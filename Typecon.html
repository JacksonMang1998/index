Typecon

2022 | Branding, Print


Typecon is an annual conference of type and design held by the Society of Typographic Aficionados (SOTA).
The concept Transcend was developed as a team to represent how experimentation, abstraction, and technology have advanced typography and broken typographic norms. In such a process of transcendence, there is change, and the change-especially that in technology or algorithms-is trial and error. Hence, Transcend experiments with the concept of glitch along with change. Furthermore, the featuring design speakers are Daniel Wenzel, Natasha Lucas, Talia Cotton, and Kyuha Shim.
