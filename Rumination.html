Rumination


2022 | Print

The Rumination Postcards is a set of postcards based on four select questions from poet Pablo Neruda's " The Book of Question." In honor of Neruda's work, the backside of the postcards is his biography

Based on the concept of "rumination," these postcards explore a deep thought and distress-- the process of continuously thinking the same thoughts. However, each postcard is designed typographically, enhancing the visual and emotional strength of each question/ though.

This set of postcards also comes with an extra singular postcard and an individual backing for letter usage.
